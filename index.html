SociaLite

A feature-rich social network app powered by Android Jetpack and Gemini AI

About SociaLite

SociaLite is an Android app that demonstrates how to use various Android platform APIs to implement features commonly seen in social network apps. It leverages a variety of Jetpack APIs to achieve complex functionality that works reliably on more devices and requires less code.

Download SociaLite

Key Features

  • Timeline of photos and videos from chats
  • Chat threads with animal avatars powered by Gemini AI
  • In-app camera for capturing photos and videos
  • Video editing capabilities
  • Notification system for chat replies
  • Settings for app customization

Technologies Used

  • Kotlin
  • Android Jetpack (Compose, Media3, CameraX, Room, Window)
  • Core Notifications
  • Google AI Client SDK (Gemini 1.5 Flash)
